OUR OBJECTIVE


The conference aims to provide unified platform for multidisciplinary research. The scope of the conference is to provide an opportunity forresearchers, Faculty members, UG and PG students of Academic and Research Institutes to share their findings and improve their knowledge inemerging technologies. Authors are requested to submit the abstract by scanning the QRCode given in the brochure or by visiting the link given in the conference website before the deadline.
